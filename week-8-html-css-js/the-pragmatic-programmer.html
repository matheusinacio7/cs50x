The Pragmatic Programmer

20th Anniversary Edition

Your Journey to Mastery
by David Thomas and Andrew Hunt

This book is one of the (if not the) best starting points I can think of. The authors, two very experienced software engineers (over 30 years when they wrote the second edition!), give many best practices recommendations, about coding, professional attitude and many other topics

The anniversary edition was heavily edited and upgraded, so I recommend it.
